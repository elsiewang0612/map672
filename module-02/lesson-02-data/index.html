Mapping, an "eye" with unlimited potential of sight

Mapping is a representation of the real world. it is a method to overcome the limitation of the human eye. We only able to see the object shown in front of us. But it couldn't satisfy our brain. However, we are able to collect the images through movement and cooperation. Then, we combine and organize them in any way we want them to be. That is the art of mapping. Therefore, we created another "eye" to help us see more.
