Major Cities of East South Area in China

Shanghai, Nanjing and Hangzhou

Map authored by Xi Wang

Additional information about the data and map goes here.

This map presents the east south area of China. Shanghai, Nanjing and Hangzhou are three major cities in this area based on the size, population and the level of development. In this map, these three cities are identified by blue markers. Not only the locations of the cities are presented, also the basic information of each of the cities are provided when clicks the markers. Shanghai has the largest population. It is the business center of China. Although Beijing is the capital city of China in terms of politics, Shanghai is another 'capital' which is leading the economy development. The coordinate, population of the cities are from Google Search and Map.
