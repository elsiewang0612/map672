Population Density of Major Cities in Southeast of China

Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou

Map authored by Xi Wang

The coordinate, population and area of the cities are searched from Google Map and Google Search.

Shanghai, Nanjing and Hangzhou are three major city in the southeast area of China. In this map, locations of three cities will be marker when the "map-cities" bottom is clicked. Each marker will show the information of cities including name and population density. In most cases, population density would be more convincing than population or area especially when doing research about optimization and pattern analysis.
